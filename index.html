About Me

Hello there! I'm Toussaint Turnier, I am a Haitian-Taiwanese American living in New York City. Who's a dedicated and enthusiastic software developer with a passion for problem-solving. Over the past decade, I've honed my skills in the ever-evolving world of software development, and I'm excited to show you what I can offer. My journey began with a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from Albany State University, where I developed a profound interest in algorithms and data structures. I swiftly followed up my studies with work, tackling complex problems in software development for both startups and established tech giants. In my software development career, I've gained experience with a wide range of programming languages, frameworks, and tools, including but not limited to Java, Python, C++, JavaScript, Ruby, React, AngularJS, Node.js, and Docker. I have a particular knack for backend development and database design, though I pride myself on being a versatile developer capable of working across the stack. But my skills go beyond just coding. I truly believe that great software is built by great teams, so I'm always working to foster a positive, collaborative, and productive environment wherever I work. Clear communication, empathy, and mutual respect are core values of mine, and I aim to bring them into every project I'm a part of. My work philosophy revolves around the Japanese concept of "Kaizen," or "continuous improvement." With every line of code I write, I strive to learn something new and make the next one better. I embrace challenges as opportunities for growth and am always open to feedback. In my free time, I love to further develop my skills through coding side-projects, contributing to open-source projects, and constantly learning about emerging technologies. Additionally, I'm an avid reader of technology and software development blogs and enjoy participating in local coding hackathons. Feel free to explore my portfolio on this website to see some of the projects I've worked on. If you believe my skills could be a good fit for your project or team, don't hesitate to get in touch. I'm always eager to connect and explore new opportunities. Let's build something amazing together!
